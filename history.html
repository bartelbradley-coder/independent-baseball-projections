Transparency Standards
  • Every posted pick is logged before first pitch.
  • Model probability, market probability, odds, edge, stake, and timestamp are stored at posting.
  • Results are not deleted after losses.
  • Closing prices are captured for validation.
  • Live results are reported separately from historical backtests.
  • Past performance does not guarantee future results.
Verify it yourself: the CSV button below the filters exports the rows currently shown — date, posted time, odds, edge, model probability, result, P&L. Leave the filters on "All" (the default) to export the complete log, and diff it against any snapshot of this page: rows are added, never removed. Independent daily snapshots of this data live at the Internet Archive — third-party copies we cannot edit.
Record notes — known imperfections in this data
  • Posting timestamps begin June 1, 2026. Picks before that date were logged pre-pitch but predate the visible-timestamp system, so no 📌 posted-time appears on those rows.
  • The posting threshold tightened over the season. Earlier picks were posted at lower edge minimums; the current bar is 4pp. Everything ever posted stays in the record — rows below today's threshold are marked "Below" rather than removed.
  • Close-based validation is not currently published. Live closing-price capture matured during the season and early closes were partly reconstructed from historical odds; validated close-based metrics will return once genuine live-close coverage accumulates.
