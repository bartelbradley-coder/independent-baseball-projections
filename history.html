🧠 What Kelly Size Means
How the model allocates confidence
1.5u+ picks
Model and market agree on a substantial pricing gap. Multiple factors align, edge is large and unambiguous.
0.5–1.5u picks
Solid edge with a meaningful signal. Standard-confidence plays that represent the bulk of the model's recommendations.
< 0.5u picks
Modest edge above threshold. Lower confidence — useful for tracking but sized small intentionally.
