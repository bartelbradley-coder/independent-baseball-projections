What is a fair line?
The theoretical price at which neither side has an edge — the true implied probability converted to American odds with no margin. Independent Baseball Projections computes this from its model probability.
How is edge calculated?
Edge is the difference between the model's win probability and the market's implied probability (after removing the vig). Independent Baseball Projections only posts picks with at least 4% edge vs. Pinnacle's no-vig line.
Does past performance guarantee future results?
No. Sports betting involves risk and variance. The model identifies positive expected value — not certainty. Never bet more than you can afford to lose.
