▶ How to read these picks
Model Prob
The model's estimate of the true win probability for this team, blended 75% model / 25% market.
Market Prob
Pinnacle's no-vig implied probability — the sharpest publicly available market price, used as the benchmark.
Edge
Model Prob minus Market Prob. Positive edge means the model thinks this team is underpriced. Only picks ≥ 2% edge are shown.
Fair Odds
What the American moneyline odds would be if the market priced the game at the model's probability with zero vig.
Break-Even
The win rate you need to profit at these odds long-term. If Model Prob > Break-Even, there's positive expected value.
Half-Kelly
Recommended bet size using the Kelly Criterion at 50% fractional scaling on a 10-unit bankroll. Reduces variance vs. full Kelly.
CLV
Closing Line Value — how much the line moved toward our pick after we logged it. Positive CLV is the gold-standard signal of real edge.
Dog play
Betting an underdog (positive odds). Higher variance but higher upside — these are where sharp models often find the most value.
