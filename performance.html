🧠 Why Edge Matters
Understanding the model's advantage over naive strategies
Vig drain is unavoidable
Every bet incurs vig (typically −4% to −5% per game at US books). Naive strategies bleed this every time. You must find mispriced games to overcome it.
Closing Line Value is the key signal
Positive CLV means the market moved toward our pick after we posted it — the sharpest bettors in the world confirmed the pricing gap we found. This is the gold standard of model validation.
Selectivity compounds
We only post picks that clear a 2% edge threshold. Betting every game results in the vig drain above. Betting selectively on mispriced markets is the entire advantage.
