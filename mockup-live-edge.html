Design Notes

  • Posted edge — always the morning value, frozen in picks_log.csv, used for CLV/ROI tracking
  • Current edge — recomputed from latest 30-min model refresh with confirmed lineups
  • Delta bar — visual indicator of how much confidence has shifted (green = stable, amber = reduced, orange = below threshold, red = flipped)
  • No opposite sides — Case 4 shows the flip as a warning only; DET is never logged as a competing pick
  • Tracking integrity preserved — picks_log.csv is never modified; all CLV/ROI history reflects the posted morning edge
  • Panel is collapsed/hidden when posted and current edge are within ~0.5pp of each other (Case 1 could optionally hide the panel entirely for a cleaner card)
